Peanut Butter & Jely Pencakes

Description

Easy and healthy PBJ Pencake recipe for breakfast

Ingredients

  • 65g Oat flour
  • 1 Banana
  • 2 Eggs
  • 20g Coconut milk
  • 8g Sunflower oil
  • 20g Whey Protein
  • 20g Peanut Butter
  • 20g Jelly
  • 5g Baking powder

Steps

  1. Collect the Ingredients
  2. Create the pancake mix
  3. Preheat the pen with oil
  4. Bake the pencakes
  5. Serve with PBJ
